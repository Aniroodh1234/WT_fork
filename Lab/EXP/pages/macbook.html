MacBook Pro 14-inch

Mind-blowing. Head-turning.

Design

Stunning aluminum unibody

MacBook Pro features a refined aluminum unibody design with a sleek profile and exceptional build quality. Available in Space Gray and Silver. MagSafe 3 charging, three Thunderbolt 4 ports, HDMI, SD card slot, and headphone jack. Advanced thermal system keeps it cool under heavy workloads.

Display

14.2-inch Liquid Retina XDR

Stunning 14.2-inch Liquid Retina XDR display with 3024-by-1964 resolution, 254 pixels per inch. Extreme Dynamic Range with 1000 nits sustained brightness, 1600 nits peak brightness for HDR content. ProMotion technology with adaptive refresh rates up to 120Hz. P3 wide color gamut and True Tone.

Performance

M3 Pro or M3 Max — unprecedented power

Choose M3 Pro with up to 12-core CPU and 18-core GPU, or M3 Max with up to 16-core CPU and 40-core GPU. Neural Engine accelerates machine learning tasks. Up to 128GB unified memory. Renders complex 3D scenes, compiles code, and edits 8K video effortlessly.

Camera

1080p FaceTime HD camera

Advanced 1080p FaceTime HD camera with larger image sensor for better low-light performance. Computational video enhances your appearance in video calls. Studio-quality three-mic array with high signal-to-noise ratio and directional beamforming.

Battery

Up to 18 hours battery life

All-day battery life with up to 18 hours of video playback. Fast-charge capable with MagSafe 3 — get up to 50% charge in just 30 minutes with 96W USB-C Power Adapter. Power efficiency of Apple silicon maximizes runtime.

Connectivity

Thunderbolt 4, Wi-Fi 6E & more

Three Thunderbolt 4 ports support charging, DisplayPort, Thunderbolt 4, and USB 4. HDMI port supports 8K displays at 60Hz or 4K at 240Hz. SDXC card slot, headphone jack with high-impedance support. Wi-Fi 6E and Bluetooth 5.3 for wireless connectivity.

Storage

Massive storage options

Configure with up to 8TB of super-fast SSD storage. Choose from 512GB, 1TB, 2TB, 4TB, or 8TB to store massive libraries of photos, videos, projects, and apps with lightning-fast access speeds.

Software

macOS — powerful and intuitive

macOS delivers powerful features with an intuitive interface. Stage Manager, Spotlight search, Continuity features, and thousands of optimized apps. Works seamlessly with iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch. Regular updates add new features and security enhancements.

Why choose

The ultimate pro notebook

For developers, video editors, 3D artists, music producers, and anyone who demands the absolute best — MacBook Pro 14-inch delivers workstation-class performance in a portable package with exceptional battery life.
