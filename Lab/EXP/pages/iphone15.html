iPhone 15 Pro

Titanium. So strong. So light. So Pro.

Design

Titanium frame & refined finish

The iPhone 15 Pro features a lightweight titanium frame with a matte textured back and Ceramic Shield front. Available in Natural Titanium, Blue, White, and Black finishes. Small bezel, refined curves, and precision-machined buttons.

Display

6.1-inch Super Retina XDR

6.1-inch OLED Super Retina XDR display with ProMotion adaptive refresh up to 120Hz, HDR10, True Tone and peak brightness for HDR content. Excellent outdoor visibility and accurate color reproduction for photo and video work.

Performance

A17 Pro — Pro-level performance

Apple’s A17 Pro chip: multi-core CPU and GPU improvements with a 16-core Neural Engine. Faster app launches, pro-grade editing, and sustained performance for gaming and heavy workloads.

Camera

Pro camera system

48MP main sensor with sensor-shift optical image stabilization, 12MP ultra wide, 12MP telephoto (optical zoom up to 3× on Pro). Photonic Engine for improved low-light photos, ProRAW and ProRes support for creative workflows.

Battery

All-day battery + USB-C

All-day battery life with fast charging over USB-C. Supports MagSafe wireless charging and accessories. Optimized power efficiency thanks to A17 Pro and iOS power management.

Connectivity

5G, Wi-Fi 6E & more

5G capable, Wi-Fi 6E support, Bluetooth 5.3, NFC, Face ID, and a USB-C port for charging and high-speed data transfer. Improved antenna design for better call quality and data throughput.

Storage

Storage options

Available in 128GB, 256GB, 512GB and 1TB configurations to suit casual users through pro creators.

Software

iOS — updates & ecosystem

Ships with the latest iOS and receives long-term updates. Seamless integration with the Apple ecosystem (iCloud, FaceTime, Continuity, Handoff, AirDrop, and more).

Why choose

Made for pros and everyday users

Combines pro-level hardware with everyday ease: editing, high-frame-rate video, gaming, and photography — all in a lighter, stronger titanium body.
